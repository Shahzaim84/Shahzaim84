## 👨‍💻 About Me

<img align="right" src="https://media.giphy.com/media/qgQUggAC3Pfv687qPC/giphy.gif" width="270" />

I am a **Full-Stack Developer** and **AI Engineer** from Pakistan, building the next generation of intelligent systems — from autonomous voice agents to multi-agent orchestration pipelines.

My work sits at the intersection of **software engineering** and **artificial intelligence**. I do not just build apps — I architect systems that think, automate, and scale on their own.

- 🤖 &nbsp; Building **AI Agents** and **Multi-Agent Systems** with LangChain and LangGraph
- 🎙️ &nbsp; Deploying **Voice AI** pipelines using VAPI AI and ElevenLabs
- ⚡ &nbsp; Designing **n8n automation workflows** for real business operations
- 🧠 &nbsp; Creating **RAG pipelines** and semantic search over private knowledge bases
- 🌐 &nbsp; Shipping full-stack **MERN** web apps with AI-powered features
- 📱 &nbsp; Exploring **React Native** for cross-platform mobile experiences

<br/>

> *"I automate the complex so businesses can focus on what actually matters."*
